The Science Behind It

Streak Stakes isn't just gamification. It's behavioral economics at work.

🧠
2×
Loss Aversion is twice as powerful as equivalent gain
Kahneman & Tversky, Prospect Theory (1979)
📅
21
Days of consistent repetition to form an automatic habit loop
Phillippa Lally et al., European Journal of Social Psychology
🤝
+30%
Better performance under loss contracts vs. bonus contracts
Commitment Device research, BMC Public Health (2019)
📉
50%
Of online language learners never return after missing one week
Chen & Kizilcec (2020), TheKnowledgeAcademy (2021)
